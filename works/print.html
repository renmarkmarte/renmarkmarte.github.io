Print Design

Graphic Design, Print Design, Branding, Illustration



Creating unique designs.

At Easy Signs, I had the opportunity to provide artwork services to a diverse range of customers everyday. Through this large clientele, I was able to develop my graphic design skills to create designs specifically suited to the customer's needs and requirements, while also improving on other important skills such as time management, teamwork and providing exceptional customer service.

From Small to Large.

Easy Signs has a wide range of products from large teardrop banners to small vinyl stickers (57 products in total). Each product has it's own unqiue design requirements - for example, suitable font sizes, amount of bleed required, colours suited for the material - that I had to consider when creating my designs.

PRINT DESIGN

Graphic Design, Print Design, Branding, Illustration


At Easy Signs, I have the wonderful opportunity of providing artwork services to a diverse range of customers everyday. My job involves collaborating with our customers to bring their design visions to life on the different types of products that we sell. This includes following customer instructions and strict brand guidelines while performing outstanding customer service for companies such as LJ Hooker, Ray White, and Anytime Fitness, as well as individuals who require assistance in creating their designs. My job also involved ensuring that customer files were compatible with our wide selection of world-class printers to align with our goal of always producing quality products.

PRINT DESIGN

Graphic Design, Print Design, Branding, Illustration


At Easy Signs, I provided design and artwork services for individuals and businesses requiring signage. This involved following customer instructions and strict brand guidelines for companies such as LJ Hooker, Ray White, and Anytime Fitness, as well as creating unique and beautiful designs depending on the occassion and customer's request. Designs were created across our wide range of products - from large vinyl banners, pull up banners, and fabric flags, to small format products such as flyers, postcards, and brochures.
